The Experience

Every Detail Is Intentional

🍽️
The Kitchen

Chef Ruben Monroy's menu honors the rich tradition of authentic Mexican cuisine — elevated with contemporary technique and the finest Valley ingredients. Not Tex-Mex. Not fusion. Mexico.

🥃
The Tequila Room

One of Scottsdale's most celebrated tequila and mezcal programs. Our bartenders are educators as much as craftsmen — expect to leave with a new appreciation for agave spirits.

🍷
The Wine Program

Partnered with Sommelier and Restaurateur Thomas Kaufman, our wine list is curated to complement the bold flavors of the kitchen — Spanish, South American, and Californian selections.

🪔
The Atmosphere

Hundreds of hand-selected Moroccan lanterns. Crimson velvet drapes. A room that feels transported. The most photographed dining room in DC Ranch — and one of the most dramatic in Arizona.

🎉
Special Occasions

Birthdays, anniversaries, proposals, and celebrations find their perfect home here. Our team anticipates every detail so your evening is extraordinary from the moment you arrive.

🏡
DC Ranch Location

Located in the heart of DC Ranch Village at 85255 — Scottsdale's most elegant neighborhood. Ample parking, easy access, and the kind of address that sets the tone before you even walk in.
